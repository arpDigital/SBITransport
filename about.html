FAQ's

WHAT EXCATLY IS A COURIER SERVICE?

It is a delivery service that is similar to shipping but instead of shipping all your goods, it is delivered to you via a driver. It is to ensure that your package is delivered on time with no delays and safely to its destination without it being bumped around.

WHY WOULD WE NEED COURIER SERVICE?

Where shipping can’t cut it, couriers fill the need. In case of urgent delivery, couriers are highly effective. In case of the need for delivering large goods, courier service uses trucks. The same day delivery service and a courier service fill those needs because your package is picked up from one point to other without any stops.

HOW QUICKLY CAN YOU DELIVER A PACKAGE?

Our fastest delivery service can differ in terms of delivery time. Some of the courier service that w offer that includes a 3 hour to 5 hour delivery, the same day delivery and depending on the distance the fastest option available is a 60 minutes delivery.

WHAT IS THE LARGEST SIZE OF GOODS THAT YOU CAN DELIVER?

We deliver a huge array of items in terms of size and the biggest that we can deliver is 25ft x 8ft x 8ft. We can deliver this feat using our 26 foot box trucks with modern lift gates.

WHAT IS THE HEAVIEST GOOD THAT CAN BE DELIVERED ON THE SAME DAY?

As mention, we use our 26 feet box trucks to carry the largest goods and they can accommodate up to 5,000 kg. Anything larger than this can be delivered but proper scheduling is required.
